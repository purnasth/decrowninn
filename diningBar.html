Foods

The Restaurant Center

Hotel De Crown Inn has a roof-top restaurant that serves delicious Nepali and international cuisine. You can enjoy your meal while admiring the panoramic view of the sunrise and sunset. The hotel also has a bar and a lounge where you can relax and unwind with your favorite drink and music.

Embark on a culinary journey at Hotel De Crown Inn, where our diverse dining options cater to every palate. Fine Dining Restaurant: Experience exquisite dining in our Fine Dining Restaurant, accommodating up to 90 guests. Open from 7:00 A.M to 10:00 P.M, indulge in a delightful array of dishes crafted with precision, from hearty breakfasts to delectable dinners. Rooftop Restaurant: Elevate your dining experience at our Rooftop Restaurant, offering mesmerizing views and a seating capacity for 80 patrons. From 12:00 P.M to 10:00 P.M, savor an enchanting blend of flavors against the backdrop of the sky, creating moments to remember. Pastry, Bakery, and Coffee Bar: For a delightful interlude, visit our cozy Pastry, Bakery, and Coffee Bar with a charming capacity for 20 guests. Open throughout the day from 7:00 A.M to 10:00 P.M, relish artisanal pastries, freshly baked goods, and expertly brewed coffee in a relaxed setting. Perfect for a quick indulgence or a leisurely break.
