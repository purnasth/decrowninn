All day dining

Fine Dining Restaurant

Experience exquisite dining in our Fine Dining Restaurant, accommodating up to 90 guests. Open from 7:00 A.M to 10:00 P.M, indulge in a delightful array of dishes crafted with precision, from hearty breakfasts to delectable dinners.

All day dining

Rooftop Restaurant

Elevate your dining experience at our Rooftop Restaurant, offering mesmerizing views and a seating capacity for 80 patrons. From 12:00 P.M to 10:00 P.M, savor an enchanting blend of flavors against the backdrop of the sky, creating moments to remember.

Pastry, Bakery, and Coffee

Bake & Beans Bar

For a delightful interlude, visit our cozy Pastry, Bakery, and Coffee Bar with a charming capacity for 20 guests. Open throughout the day from 7:00 A.M to 10:00 P.M, relish artisanal pastries, freshly baked goods, and expertly brewed coffee in a relaxed setting. Perfect for a quick indulgence or a leisurely break.

Events Destination

De Crown Hall

Discover the perfect venue for your events at De Crown Inn. Our spacious hall is designed to adapt to your needs, accommodating up to 45 people in U-shape, 44 in a classroom, and 88 in theatre mode. Whether it's a meeting, workshop, or presentation, our versatile space ensures a seamless and tailored experience for your events.
